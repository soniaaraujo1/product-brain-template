# To-Do & Parking Lot

The operational layer — one place for the things that otherwise get lost in meeting notes and chats.
This is **not** Jira and **not** the risk register:

- **Jira** = formal product work items (story cards, bugs in the build).
- **`Risks-and-Assumptions.md`** = things to figure out or watch.
- **This file** = things to *do* (follow-ups, reminders) and ideas to *revisit*.

`/capture-meeting` routes action items here. Triage it regularly, or it rots like every other to-do list.
