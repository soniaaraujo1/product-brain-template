# Working in Customer-Feedback/

- Raw voice of the customer: requests, interview/call notes, feedback themes. It **informs** the
  roadmap; it is not the roadmap.
- File items with `_TEMPLATE.md`. Capture the underlying **need**, not just the requested feature.
- Group recurring asks into `theme-<topic>.md`; individual notes `<account-or-date>-<topic>.md`.
- Link to `Modules/…`; graduate ideas via the `To-Do.md` parking lot or `/shape-idea`.
